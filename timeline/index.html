On March 3rd and 4th, we officially hosted Hacklahoma, Oklahoma’s first student-run hackathon! Over 200 students attended, with 47 total projects submitted. View the 2018 website

The 2018 logo was created to show our hopes for growth and innovation in the tech community in Oklahoma. Thus we illustrated this using a plant sprouting above an integrated circuit, its roots represented as nodes.
