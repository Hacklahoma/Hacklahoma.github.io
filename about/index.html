Who We Are

We are a group of students from the University of Oklahoma who are passionate about fostering innovation and collaboration in Oklahoma’s tech industry. Today, our team consists of 12 individuals across 4 subteams - Marketing, Operations, Sponsoring & Finance, and Technology.

What We Do

Hacklahoma’s mission is to kindle creativity and innovation in Oklahoma and develop a thriving tech community. We strive to be an organization where all of our team members feel included and have opportunities to gain experience and grow.
